#5things you can do to save the environment

Read an article

Educating yourself on environmental and social issues helps you develop a better understanding of the world. Keep informed about on nature, endangered species and successes of our volunteers around the globe.

Share the news

Spreading awareness of environmental issues can help people, animals and local flora in the endangered areas. Amplify voices of those in need and use your social media channels for change. Click the button below to share an article to Facebook.

Support the cause

We understand that not everyone can afford expensive donations. It can also be time-consuming to make sure the money goes to the right place. But even a couple of kroner can help us - MobilePay 5kr for a cause of your choice right now by clicking the button below.

Show-off

We challenge you to find a bunch of reusable items you have in your flat. Bottles, tote bags, cutlery... anything else? Take a selfie with your reusables to show others that you can be cute and produce less waste at the same time.

Cook-off

We all love a good food pic. Show us what you eat - just remember, it has to be vegan! And yes, you probably eat vegan meals and snacks every day, just have a look at the ingredients lists.
